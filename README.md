Horror City – Welcome, Hero

Horror City lets visitors live heroic experiences, face terrifying challenges, and enjoy cinematic adventures with interactive events that turn every guest into the hero of their story.

Part of the 7Worlds Empire, Horror City features seven unique zones, each designed for a distinct type of fear: Shadows, Screams, Abyss, Hallucination, Blood, Escape, and Ghost Walk. Visitors earn courage points in each zone, tracked via the 7Worlds app, transforming them from spectators into cinematic heroes.

Built with: Azure, React, AWS, Google Cloud, JavaScript, MySQL, Kubernetes, MongoDB, Node.js, OpenAI API, Python, Docker, Web3.

Next steps: seasonal story updates, VIP experiences, connection to the larger Horror Island, live escape-film adventures, and Horror City eSports challenges.
